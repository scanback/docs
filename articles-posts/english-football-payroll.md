Club payroll spending on football is well known. But how much does this correspond to relative to city economy size? I doubt anyone knows.  

I took club 2025-26 payroll data provided by [Capology], aggrgated up the clubs to their corresponding cities (UN definition), and combined this with disposable income of the lower middle class and above (the typical consumers).
